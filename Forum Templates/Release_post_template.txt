[B]Features:[/B]

Memory leak fixes and other fixes from AOSP.
Layers 2.1 Support.
Notification Ticker.
Statusbar Brightness Control.
Show Notification Count.
Double-tap Statubar or Navbar to sleep.
Statusbar Clock Customization.
Headsup Swipe.
Battery Status Style.
Privacy Guard.
Blacklist.
Filter Notifications.
Smart Call.
Smart Mute.
Detailed Caller Information.
Phone Number Lookup.
Quick Pin Unlock.
Gesture Unlock.
Quick Settings.
Quick Pulldown.
Weather in Quick pulldown.
Ability to disable Heads up.
Force Expanded Notification.
Advanced Reboot.
Screen Record.
Clear All Recents.
Ability to adjust 'Clear recents' FAB.
Show Search Bar in Recents Menu.
LCD Density Customization.
Navbar Customization.
Navrings Customization.
Re-Orient Volume Buttons.
Expanded Desktop.
Ambient Display Customization.
Silent Mode.
Battery Light Customization.
LED Notification Customization.

[B]How to install:[/B]

Download the ROM
Copy the ROM to your SD Card
Boot into recovery
Install the ROM
Wipe data, cache and dalvik cache
Reboot
